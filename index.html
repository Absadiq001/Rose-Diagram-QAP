QAP Streckeisen Guide

The **QAP diagram** classifies plutonic rocks using modal ratios of three felsic mineral groups:

  • Q (Quartz): Plotted at the top apex.
  • A (Alkali Feldspar): Plotted at the bottom-left corner.
  • P (Plagioclase): Plotted at the bottom-right corner.

Mafic minerals are ignored. If you have raw mineral volumes or weight percentages, toggle "Raw weights", and the system will normalize them automatically ($Q+A+P=100\%$) before plotting.
